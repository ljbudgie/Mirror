WHAT YOU MUST DO NOW

1. CONTACT ACAS IMMEDIATELY

   Before you can bring a tribunal claim, you must notify ACAS and go
   through Early Conciliation. This is a legal requirement.

   ACAS helpline:    0300 123 1100
   Online:           acas.org.uk/early-conciliation

   Early Conciliation pauses the tribunal clock while ACAS tries to help
   you reach an agreement.

2. PREPARE YOUR CLAIM

   If conciliation does not resolve the issue, ACAS will issue you a
   certificate. You then have at least one calendar month from the date
   of the certificate to submit your claim to the Employment Tribunal.

3. SEEK LEGAL ADVICE

   Free advice may be available from:
   - Citizens Advice (citizensadvice.org.uk)
   - ACAS (acas.org.uk)
   - Law centres in your area

IMPORTANT NOTES

- The 3-month-minus-1-day limit is strict
- Time runs from the date of the act you are complaining about
- If you miss this deadline, you may lose the right to bring a claim
